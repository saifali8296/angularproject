Electronic Healthcare Record (EHR) is a systematic collection of record in a digital format that has electronic health information about an individual patient or population. It is a digital version of a patient’s paper chart. EHR is a real-time, patient-centered record that makes information available instantly and securely to authorized users. EHR has remarkable advantages over paper records. It tracks data over time, Monitor and improve overall quality of care within the practice.
